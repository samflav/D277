Philadelphia

Philadelphia, Pennsylvania's largest city, is a vibrant cultural and historical hub known as the birthplace of American democracy. Home to iconic landmarks like the Liberty Bell, Independence Hall, and the Philadelphia Museum of Art, the city is steeped in history and rich in character. Philly offers a dynamic mix of colonial charm and modern energy, with thriving arts, music, and culinary scenes, including its famous cheesesteaks. Its walkable neighborhoods are filled with historic row homes, lively markets, and diverse communities.
